About him

Jacob Thureson (January 11, 2001 – June 27th, 2019), better known by his stage name Hella Sketchy

The young prodigy established himself as one of SoundCloud’s most legendary producers by the age of 18. He amassed over 20,000 followers on his SoundCloud page and was extremely well known for his very popular “new wave” beats

He produced some of Soundcloud rap’s biggest hits for Ugly God, Tay-K, Wintertime, Lil Tracy, PnB Rock, Warhol.ss, Pollàri, and a crowd of other artists, many of these while still only 16 or 17. He also worked with other well-known producers such as Charlie Shuffler, Ginseng and Diablo. As a rapper, he found early success with his viral hit “Spent A Check,” the first of his songs to achieve over 1,000,000 views on YouTube. A few of his songs have benefited from music videos posted to large YouTube channels (i.e. “Stupid” and “Llama”).

He was a part of the SoundCloud super-group D1, which consisted of Ginseng’s rap alias Lil Wintr, producer Shiro, producer Despair, rapper Marcelo, and Hella Sketchy himself.On June 15th, 2019, Hella Sketchy’s sister reported he was put into a coma after overdosing on drugs, implying in another Tweet that the rapper-producer’s condition was brought on by opioids specifically. On June 27th, 2019, his father reported he had passed away early in the morning after 14 days of intensive care in a comatose state.
